Privacy Policy

App Name: Quick Voice Recorder | Last Updated: January 2024

At Quick Voice Recorder, developed by Ankit Parmar, we recognize that privacy is important. This document outlines the types of information we receive and collect when you use the app.

1. Information Collection

Microphone Data: The app requires access to the device's microphone to perform its core function (audio recording). Audio data is processed in real-time and saved directly to your device.

Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. Data Storage & Security

All recordings created using Quick Voice Recorder are stored locally on your device's internal storage or SD card. We do not have access to your recordings, and they are never uploaded to any external servers or cloud services.

3. Third-Party Services

The app currently operates without internet requirements, meaning no data is shared with third-party analytics or advertisement networks. Your data remains completely offline.

4. User Consent

By using the app and granting the requested permissions (Microphone and Storage), you consent to the processing of data as described in this policy.
